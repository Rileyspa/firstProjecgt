Bread

White Bread

ingredients
  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4T salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettel, Large mixing bowl, Large mixing spoon (wooden), 4 cup glass measruring cup, plasic wrap, dish

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettel and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cup of all your dry ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into a large measruring cup. If you don't have a large measruring cup, just combine them.
  4. Start adding the 3 cup of hot water. When you only have about half a cup left, feel the water and keep stiring.
  5. Mix the liquids ito the flour with a large spoon.
  6. Pour the remaining flour in to the bowl and keep stiring if it gets to hard add more water.
  7. Keep adding flour until it is at a consistency where it is no longer sticking to your fingers.
  8. Kned it for about 10 mitnutes.You can't over kned it when you do it by hand.
  9. Stop when you press on it and it spring back.
  10. Pour oil around the edge of the bowl spin the dough and cover the dough on the oil.
  11. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap then a towel
  12. If your in a hurry then put in a warm place.
  13. After it has doubled is size, form it how you want it.
  14. Let it rise for another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degrees in the oven for 20 minutes.
